STEAM Camp

Why STEAM

STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Math) emphasizes the bridge between scientific-based thinking and creative implementation. Art has been shown to improve innovation and overall cognitive development in children.

Our Project

We are a group of girls who's goal is to aim for spreading awareness in not just STEM, but STEAM. We want to inspire youth to go beyond the boundaries of pure logical or analytical thinking and mix in their creative talents. To do this, we hosted week-long STEAM camps for elementary and middle school-aged students to engage in various hands-on projects. This website serves as a resource for thoses who are interested in incorporating STEAM ideas into their programs and activites.
