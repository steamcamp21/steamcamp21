About STEAM 15

STEAM 15 is a program that focuses on bringing STEAM education directly to kids through camps with hands on activities. Each day hones in on a new aspect of STEAM, allowing kids to dabble in a new topic. Starting out as a Gold Award project, STEAM 15 hopes to reach out even more kids through interactive activities and continue to share the value of science, technology, engineering, art, and math with youth.
